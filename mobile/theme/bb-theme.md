# BB Theme (derived from website)

## Core colors
- Pink: `#ff2bd6`
- Gold: `#ffb000`
- Cyan: `#00e7ff`
- Deep purple: `#2a0030`
- Background dark: `#07020A`

## Visual style
- High contrast, playful "sticker" blocks with bold borders.
- Punchy gradient accents (pink/purple/gold).
- Bold headings and labels.
- Status badges for ONLINE/CONNECTED states.

## Component guidance
- Primary button: pink background, black text, heavy font.
- Secondary button: white background, thick black border.
- Cards: dark card with glowing border accent.
- Badges: rounded pill + high-saturation fill.


## Logo usage
- Primary logo asset for app UI: `mobile/assets/logo.png`
- Use on splash, welcome, and settings/about header.
- Keep aspect ratio; do not stretch.
- Prefer 24px+ clear space around the logo in top-level screens.
